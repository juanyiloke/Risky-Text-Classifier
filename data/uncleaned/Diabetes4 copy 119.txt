BBC RE 'DO YOU BUY 'SUITABLE FOR DIABETICS' FOODBBC News and Current Affairs are searching for people who currently buy, or have had bought for them, foods labelled as ‘suitable for diabetics’ or ‘diabetic’ chocolates, jams, biscuits and more. Do you buy them? Have you had them bought as a present?  If you’d be interested in talking to a member of the team about your experience of these foods and giving your opinion – please contact producer Robert Murray at the BBC for a chat. You’d be under no obligation to take part and it would be an initial, private conversation, unless you agreed to take part in the film at a later date. You can contact Robert on: "robert .murray 01 @ bbc .co .uk" or call: 0161 335 7536
