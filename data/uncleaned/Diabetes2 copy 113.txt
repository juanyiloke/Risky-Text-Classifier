That is, until my last 2 dilations in May 2005 and March 2006 which revealed no retinopathy. The ophthalmologist explained that sometimes &quot;they just reabsorb&quot; and attributed the good news to good diabetes control. My endo said &quot;congrats.&quot; I'm elated. Not finding much on spontaneous cure, but until now, I thought the best news I could get was &quot;no change.&quot; Please do not publish my email address.
